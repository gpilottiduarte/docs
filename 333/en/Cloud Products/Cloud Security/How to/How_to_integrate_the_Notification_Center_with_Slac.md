# How to integrate the Notification Center with Slack

In this document, you’ll learn how to integrate the notification center with Slack, receiving all types of notifications directly on Slack.

The integration with Slack is a tenant-level feature, so you’ll only receive notifications for the tenants you integrated.

## Requirements

- A Slack webhook URL. For more information on how to create a webhook, see [Sending messages using incoming webhooks](https://api.slack.com/messaging/webhooks).

## Create an integration between the Notification Center and Slack

1. Access your desired product.  
2. In the top bar, select the tenant you want to integrate with Slack.  
3. In the top bar, click the **Notifications** button, represented by the bell icon.  
4. On the **Notifications** sidebar, click on the Slack icon.  
5. Click on **+ Add**.  
6. In the **Channel name** field, enter the name of the Slack channel.  
7. In the **URL** field, enter the webhook URL.  
8. In the **Notification type** field, select the types of notifications you want to receive on Slack.  
9. Click on **Save**.

To add more channels, follow the tutorial above starting from step 4.

## Edit an integration between the Notification Center and Slack

1. Access your desired product.  
2. In the top bar, select the tenant you want to integrate with Slack.  
3. In the top bar, click the **Notifications** button, represented by the bell icon.  
4. On the **Notifications** sidebar, click on the Slack icon.  
5. Click on the **Edit** button of the desired integration.  
6. In the **Channel name** field, enter the Slack channel.  
7. In the **URL** field, enter the webhook URL.  
8. In the **Notification type** field, select the types of notifications you want to receive on Slack.  
9. Click on **Save**.
