# Private SaaS

### **What is the Private SaaS?**

The Private SaaS model by senhasegura provides a dedicated cloud infrastructure for each client. This offering is tailored for organizations requiring heightened control, customization, and security within their cloud environment.

### **Features**

* **Dedicated Cloud Infrastructure**: each client receives a unique and isolated cloud environment, ensuring tailored configurations and security measures.  
* **Customizable Geographically Distributed Architectures**: clients can configure their cloud architecture across various global regions according to their specific needs and strategies.  
* **Client-specific NTP, DNS, Domain, and Digital certificates**: allows for the use of the client's preferred network time protocol, domain name servers, custom domains, and digital certificates.  
* **Closed console with Site-to-Site VPN**: provides secure console access through a VPN, enhancing privacy and mitigating risks.  
* **Client-configured backups**: enables clients to design and manage their backup protocols to fit organizational requirements and policies.

### **Applicability**

The Private SaaS model is ideally suited for organizations needing high customization, control, and security levels. It’s especially beneficial for those operating in highly regulated industries or with complex IT governance requirements.
